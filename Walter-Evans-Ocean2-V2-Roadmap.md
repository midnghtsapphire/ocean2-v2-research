**3. INVESTOR PITCH OUTLINE**

*   **The Problem:** Energy transmission from deep-ocean sites is prohibitively expensive ($30M+/km for cabling).
*   **The Solution:** Ocean-2 V2—the "Bobbing Ball" WEC with onboard Green Hydrogen conversion.
*   **The Edge:** Modular design, TRL 7 readiness, and a proven 50 KW prototype in Puget Sound.
*   **The Market:** $100B+ global Blue Economy and the rapidly growing green hydrogen sector.
*   **The Ask:** $5M Seed round for a 500 KW full-scale pilot deployment.
