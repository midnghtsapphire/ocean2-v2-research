# Brand Guidelines — Ocean2 V2

## Brand position
Ocean2 V2 is positioned as a practical deep-ocean clean-energy platform focused on reliability, modular scale, and measurable environmental responsibility.

## Voice
- Clear, evidence-based, engineering-first
- Ambitious but non-hyped
- Climate-positive and implementation-oriented

## Core message pillars
1. **Harvest untapped wave energy** in deep-ocean environments.
2. **Solve transfer constraints** with modular system design and export pathways.
3. **Scale responsibly** with ecosystem monitoring and transparent reporting.

## Visual direction
- Primary palette: deep ocean blue, aqua, slate, and white contrast
- Imagery: wave dynamics, offshore deployments, modular engineering detail
- Diagram style: high-contrast, annotation-first, investor-readable

## Naming and terminology
- Preferred: “Ocean2 V2”, “wave energy converter”, “deep-ocean deployment”, “modular arrays”
- Avoid: unsupported superlatives or unverified performance claims
